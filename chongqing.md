# Chongqing and Sichuan

## Big Cities

### Chongqing

- hotpot!
- the viral places + WWII memories
- the fine arts academy
- hot springs hotel

### Chengdu

- pandas (red pandas too), fancy city, nice old houses and temples (recommended if you love Tale of Three Kingdoms)
- tea houses are very nice and relaxing

## Smaller cities

### Leshan

- the giant buddha
- numerous tasty foods: beef hotpot (not spicy at all), sweet roasted duck, egg cake, insta-style local chain

### Yibin

- Li-Zhuang well-preserved old town
- very nice non-spicy foods: bbq skewers, mushroom soup, savoury noodles, sweet buns
- Origin town for wuliangye bai jiu (second only to Moutai)
